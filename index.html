Train and Pray

To raise children who excel in academics and deliberately taught to love God.

Our vision: To demonstrate our motto "Train and Pray" by combining sound,academics training and Godly knowledge. To run a school where God reigns and rules supreme and pleasing Him counts.

Our mission: To raise children who excel in academics can are deliberately taught to love God. To maintain good academic standards at all times by having quality staff always, combined with good supervision of children's performances. To inculcate in children a love for education and a fear of God that build them into mature, succcessful adults and dependable citizens of our nation. Conclusion is to produce an end-time army of God whose members are academically, spiritually, physically and mentally sound. These are exemplary future leaders who will be successful in every endeavour and positively influence our ever-changing world.
