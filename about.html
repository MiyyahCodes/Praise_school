BRIEF HISTORY OF PRAISE SCHOOL

The School was established on the 27th of January 1987 with the name "Tey Praise Nursery School". The "TEY" was coined out of Mrs. P.Y. Olulode (Mummy School) name "YETUNDE".


Tey Praise Nursery School was given a Provisional Approval to open a Nursery School on 14th August 1987 by Oyo State Ministry of Education.


Tey Praise Nursery School was changed to praise Nursery School in 1990.


The maiden Inter - House Sports Competition of the School was held on 28th February 1992 at The Lekan Salami Sports Complex, Adamasingba, Ibadan.


Praise Nursery School was given Provisional Approval for upgrading to Primary School on 18th March 1993 by the Oyo State Ministry of Education.


The first Headmaster of the School was Late Mr Tayo Bolarinwa. He left the office as the new Headmaster with immediate effect.


The following people were the Past Praise School Administrators:

Mrs 'Seye Sodeke (Nee Masominu) of blessed memory.

Mrs. O. B. A. Taiwo.

Mrs. L. K. Diyan.


Mrs. O. B. A. Taiwo handed over to Mrs. A. N. Akinyemi on 3rd January 2008 as the School new Administrators till date.


The School has been running for 35years and children kept coming every session. This is a wonder from a wonderful God.


The products of the School are making waves in different fields.


BRIEF HISTORY OF PRAISE ACADEMY

The school resumed oon 17th of September, 2007. The first Principal was Mrs. T. A. Adeyemi.


The L.I.E, South West Mr. Babayemi and Mr. I. A. Kareem and the Ministry of Health had carried out an inspection to the school and made useful suggestions. Five teachers pioneered the academic activities in the school.


The Proprietress, Mrs. P. Y. Olulode responded to God's mandate to start a school and Government's directive that the 6-3-3-4 policy be put in place spurred her into starting the secondary school since there was a primary school in existence.


The state BECE took place on 17th of February, 2009 while the NECO accreditation for JSCE took place on the 28th of October, 2009. On the 18th of October, 2009. On the 18th of October, 2011, Praise Academy Phase II was commissioned amidst pomp and gaiety. It ws an occasion that gave glory to God. The school was accredited to start NECO and WEAC SSCExamiation on the 22nd October, 2012 and 20th November, 2012 respectively.

The 12 students christened the Apostles were the first set of students to be registered for WEAC.


Today, the school seats on acres of land in a serene and conducive environment off Ring Road, Akinyemi Way, Ibadan, Oyo State. It boasts of a Library, Food and Nutrition Laboratory, Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Agricultural Science Laboratories. It has an enviable Computer Room which is well equipped. The classes are spacious and it has a low student-teacher ratio. These are very advantageous factors to learning.


All the teachers are qualified in their various fields, specialized and highly impactful. Relevant teaching aids are available to aid learning. Teaching and learning takes place in a Godly environment.


The products of the school have excelled in various fields.
